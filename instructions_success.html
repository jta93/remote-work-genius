How to Use the Remote Work Success Kit

Getting Started

Download and open the Excel workbook. Each sheet is labelled for its purpose.

Content Calendar

Use this sheet to plan your blog posts, videos or social media content. Fill in your ideas, target keywords and channels, and update the status as you publish.

Affiliate Tracker

List affiliate programs you join. Note the commission rates, application links, and any special terms.

Financial Tracker

Record every income and expense. Categorise each entry and regularly review the totals.

Productivity Planner

Plan your daily tasks in hourly blocks. Include high‑priority tasks first and mark their status when completed.

Goals Planner

Set your goals and track your progress. Adjust time frames as needed to stay on track.

Tools & Apps

Refer to this sheet for recommended tools and apps to boost your productivity and manage your business.

Contact Tracker

Keep track of outreach efforts, clients, or potential partners. Record follow‑up dates to maintain relationships.

Consult the Guide

The accompanying guide provides best practices and strategies for remote work success. Use both resources together for maximum benefit.
